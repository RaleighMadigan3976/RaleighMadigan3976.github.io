Reptiles

Two species - an ordinary snake and a tiger python - only seven specimens, kept in terrariums of different pavilions, mainly in the "House of Birds" and "Aquarium". Sometimes, later, during certain exhibitions, the number of reptiles in the Planet Zoo reached 10 species and 50 specimens.

Birds

In the vastness of the park there is a river, groves, glades, and even "mountains" in the rock garden. More than 2000 animals, birds, reptiles and fish from different parts of the world live here.

Animals

A class of vertebrates, the main distinguishing feature of which is feeding the cubs with milk. According to biologists, from 5000 to 5500 modern species of mammals are known, including the species Homo sapiens (lat. Homo sapiens); the total number of mammalian species, including extinct ones, exceeds 20,000.

Welcome to Zoo Planet

By the time the zoo was opened, it contained 134 specimens of domestic animals, 153 specimens of wild animals and birds, and 7 specimens of reptiles. The Russian fauna was best represented: bears, wolves, foxes, badgers, ferrets, martens, hares, squirrels, hedgehogs, deer, falcons, hawks, owls, owls, bustards, cranes, herons, ducks. The creators of the garden wanted to show the audience, first of all, our native nature. There were also exotic animals on display - 2 lions, a jaguar, 2 tigers, a leopard, a rhino, an alligator.
